Frequently asked

Where is my data stored?

Entirely on your device. Daylark doesn't use accounts or servers, and nothing you enter is sent to us. That also means we can't see or recover your data — so keep your device backed up.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Daylark works fully offline. It doesn't need an internet connection.

How does voice input work?

When you tap the microphone, your speech is turned into text using Apple's on-device speech recognition. Nothing is recorded or sent anywhere. You can allow or deny the permission, and the app works either way.

Will you add more features?

Yes — updates are planned. If there's something you'd like to see, email us; feedback shapes what comes next.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual items in the app, use the reset option in Settings, or delete the app entirely, which removes all of its data from your device.
